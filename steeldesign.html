STRUCTURAL STEEL DESIGN

Project Location: Evanston, Illinois

Project Completed: June 2015

School: Northwestern University

Course: Structural Steel Design

Instructor: Karen Chou

Collaborator: Bruno Saffi

This project required the structural design of a octagonal-shaped, two floor Data Center Building. Gravity loads due to mechanical/data equipment and lateral loads due to wind had to be accounted for. The structural elements utilized were typical steel beams, girders, and columns. The elements were designed by thoroughly analyzing for tension, compression, flexure, shear, and deflections in the elements.
