SOCIAL HOUSING

Project Location: Barrio El Raval, Barcelona

Project Completed: January 2016

Instructor: Mara Partida

Collaborator:Barbara Valdivia

The project requires the design of a social housing complex with community-style housing units and two public spaces; a library and co-working space. The prevailing idea behind the project was to create permeability and accessibility. Informed by the urban texture of the neighborhood, the arrangement of the four volumes opens up a large central green space for the surrounding community. The buildings themselves are permeable to natural light and air with exterior vertical louvers as the main facade in order to blur the line between the natural world outside and domestic world inside.
