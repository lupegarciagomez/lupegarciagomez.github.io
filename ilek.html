ILEK ON ICE

Workshop Location: Stuttgart, Germany

Workshop Completed: September 2015

School: Institute for Lightweight Structures (ILEK)

Instructors: Irina Auernhammer, Oliver Gericke, Daria Kovaleva,

Collaborators: Various students from Northwestern University and the University of Stuttgart

In this workshop we studied formwork and surface techniques for concrete structures by experimenting with sand and ice. We had the opportunity to show case our work with world-renowned structural engineering Warner Sobek. He was delighted and impressed with our work in form-finding.
